  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
    I want the players to have a good time with their peers while playing this game as they all get to go through with different combinations of exercises and the person that clicks on the most difficult exercise will have trouble going through with the exercise while the rest of the people will enjoy watching the person exercise.
  • Gameplay: A paragraph describing the actions the player can perform, the system dynamics, and the core mechanic. Include a concise explanation of the prototype’s inputs and their expected effects (how to play). You can also describe game play that is not in the prototype. You may include mock-up images for parts of the game not in the prototype.
    In this prototype, the player can perform multiple actions like pushups, jumping jacks, and squats. The first two rows contain exercises and the next two rows contain quantities. The players would just click on on card from the top half and one card from the bottom half and go through with the exercise they chose the selected number of times. The game would continue until the cards are compleated. The game could be extended by adding more cards and allowing the numbers and exercises to be randomized
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?

This will be more of a concern when more cards are added to the game and the players actually have to do more than one exersice.
